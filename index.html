Starting from IOS 9, The developer has to be trusted by the device, otherwise a popup will appear and prevent using the app.

On iOS 9.0/9.1, go to Setting > General > Proviles > tap on the developer's profile, and tap on Trust.

On iOS 9.2+, go to Setting > General > Device Management > tap on the developer's profile, and tap on Trust
